Now that we understand that we and our bodies need different things from food all we need to do is to balance nutrition. OMG you'd say - it sounds like it's another website that will tell me about 2 cups of vegetables and an 8oz steak cooked without oil. No, no and no. Balanced-foods is a different kind of a website

This website does one very simple thing - it just balances your nutrition for you. You just eat what you like, what tastes the best for you and the website automatically shows you the right amount of each food in your meal and proper substitutions for foods if the meal otherwise can't be balanced

Balanced-foods treats nutrition balancing problem as an algorithmic/computational problem. It works like this

1. You specify your nutrition needs. That's like this: less than 2000 calories a day, at least 100g of protein, less than 2500Mg of sodium etc

2. You submit details about your daily meal - with all foods and amounts

3. And balanced-foods immediately shows you the current balance of nutrients (that's easy)

4. Then it suggests how you have to change the amount of each food to reach your perfect balance as near as possible (that's not easy at all)

5. And finally it scans a huge database of 400000 USDA foods and for each food in your meal finds the best substitution that is on the one hand close to the food in terms of minerals, vitamins and the taste and on the other hand has missing pieces of a puzzle (or doesn't have bad pieces of a puzzle) to balance your nutritions. That's absolutely impossible for a human to do manually

On top of that balanced-foods can help you

1. Find a right balance for nutrients for your specific diet e.g. keto diet, low carb diet, low fat diet, high protein diet etc

2. Include a proper amount of vitamins and milerals in your food according to your needs

3. Find out which food in your diet is responsible for which fat gain. For example did you know that one bite of an apple pie each day is responsible for 1 pound of fat gain in 3 months?

4. Predict your weight, muscle and fat gain over time with your current diet and also help change your diet to stay in shape, but without refusal of your favorite dishes

5. And finally it will help your body find foods with exact amount of protein, fat, carbs, calories and micronutrients

Believe it or not but it does not matter what you like to eat - healthy high protein foods or fast food or sweets. In every case there is a way to balance your meal and feed your body with enough minerals and vitamins, stay on a caloric balance or even deficit, do not gain weight even if you have a sedentary lifestyle and still enjoy the taste of all your favorite dishes. Now your next step would be to change food amounts manually or add some new food manually or try to substitute foods manually. Right?

It depends on how is annoying you to do those things manually. If it's a lot then here is what Balanced-Foods is for. Submit your food and let us take care about changing amounts or finding substitutions in your diet to stay in the perfect balance with your body needs
